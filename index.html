About our project

Our project is about fire and smoke. We are making a drone with smoke sensor and tempeture sensor on it main reason to do this to detect fire inside forest. As we all know that while forest fire we cant detect it directly because of trees this is why we are providing you a drone with all sensor to detect the fire in early stage and stop the fire before it spreads
